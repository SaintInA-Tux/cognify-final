Why IBP?
No inner derivative for substitution. Product of two unrelated function types → IBP with u = algebraic, dv = exponential.
Formula or definition
Topic definition
Integration by Parts reverses the product rule. Used when the integrand is a product of two functions that don't simplify via substitution.
Formula
∫u dv = uv − ∫v du

ILATE: Inverse trig → Log → Algebraic → Trig → Exponential
